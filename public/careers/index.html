Come work for the undisputed market leader.

Nathan Airchime is proud of our status as the nation's premier supplier of locomotive air horns. We have earned that status through an unwavering dedication to precision, and putting the customer first. We offer you the opportunity to grow your career while working for a company that makes things that matter.

Why Nathan Airchime?

Nathan Airchime is a company with a long history of success, an outstanding present, and a bright future. We are America’s largest producer of Airhorns — a critical component of railroad safety. We pride ourselves on providing you with opportunities for personal and professional growth and satisfaction. We encourage continuous improvement — for our products and our employees.

At Nathan Airchime, we value a diverse, inclusive workforce and we provide equal employment opportunity for all applicants and employees. All qualified applicants for employment will be considered without regard to an individual’s race, color, sex, gender identity, gender expression, religion, age, national origin or ancestry, citizenship, physical or mental disability, medical condition, family care status, marital status, domestic partner status, sexual orientation, genetic information, military or veteran status, or any other basis protected by federal, state or local laws. If you are unable to submit your application because of incompatible assistive technology or a disability, please contact us at hr@nautilus.com. Nathan Airchime will reasonably accommodate qualified individuals with disabilities to the extent required by applicable law.

Our Values

At Nathan Airchime, we're proud of our ability to provide precision products to customers with the highest level of expectations. We understand their importance – and to your contribution to their excellence.

Culture

Nathan Airchime works hard to promote a culture of respect, ethics, mutual respect in an atmosphere of goodwill. We're small enough to promote a collaborative work environment that is both casual and customer oriented. To that end, we offer flexible working hours, and, where possible, the option of remote work.

Training & Mentorship

At Nathan Airchime, you benefit from our membership in the American Management Association and our connection with EWIB (Eastern Connecticut Workforce Investment Board) Our business is guided by input, insights and training from CONNSTEP – Connecticut's leading business consulting firm.
